ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE (VESPER DALE)

VESPER DALE LIMITED is an innovative, multifaceted company duly registered with Corporate Affairs Commission. Vesper Dale carries on the business of construction, building, transportation and logistics as well as the manufacturing, buying and selling of all articles, substances, products, systems and appliances. We are general contractors.

We provide professional client-focused construction solutions and maintenance. As a result-oriented company, we go above and beyond on every project and deliver on our promises with integrity. We ensure that all clients who deal with us achieve their aims and objectives efficiently and in the best time possible. The Construction Sector of Vesper Dale is growing tremendously with services cutting across, estate development, building plan and designs, improvement and renovation of buildings, maintenance, demolition of construction works, among others.

In the execution of all our projects, we ensure the engagement of Chartered Professionals in various industries whose thoughts are focused on delivering the highest expertise and standards so as to meet our client's needs in the best way possible. Our commitment to outstanding job completion, exceptional customer service and superior safety performance has made us a partner of choice as a general contracting firm in the manufacturing, buying, selling, importing and exporting of all kind of goods and raw materials in any part of the world.
VESPER DALE LIMITED is a company of high standards and top-notch service delivery.
